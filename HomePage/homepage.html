A1 Portal is a technology service platform for the purpose of introducing advanced technology from the whole world to small and medium-sized enterprises.

A1 Portal has been officially launched since 2020 and has been in-depth strategic cooperation with Canada in high-tech R&D, incubation and industrialization. Relying on foreign expert resources and partner resources, we have established a high-quality science and technology resource pool based on science, technology and products, covering but not limited to intelligent medical technology, IT Internet, energy saving and environmental protection.

We always take innovation as the driving force for the development of science and technology and integrate Israel's cutting-edge science and technology projects with the Canadian industry, aiming to become a professional and effective technology service platform in Canada.
